Frequently Asked Questions

Flow Streaming Co., Ltd. is the company behind the Flow+ streaming service and Flow News. We are focused on delivering high-quality live TV, on-demand content, and up-to-date news through innovative and user-friendly platforms. Our aim is to make diverse entertainment and information easily accessible.

Flow+ offers a mix of live television channels spanning news, entertainment, sports, movies, and more. Additionally, we provide access to information and links for a vast library of on-demand movies and TV series through our partnership with TMDB. Flow News provides curated news articles from various sources, powered by NewsAPI.

Flow+ primarily aggregates and provides access to freely available streams and content information. We strive to offer a "zero cost" experience for the core features. Some linked third-party services or specific premium content might have their own subscription models, which are separate from Flow+.

You can browse live channels through our "Live TV" section on Flow+, categorized for easy navigation. Simply click on a channel to start streaming. Ensure you have a stable internet connection for the best viewing experience.

We partner with The Movie Database (TMDB) to provide comprehensive information for movies and TV series, including posters, ratings, and overviews. Flow+ helps you discover this content.

Flow News partners with NewsAPI to aggregate and display a wide array of news articles from reputable global sources, ensuring you have access to timely and diverse information.
